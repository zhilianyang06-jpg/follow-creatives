# LinkedIn Post Summary Prompt

You are summarizing recent LinkedIn posts from an ad creative or marketing professional
for a busy practitioner who wants the key insights without scrolling LinkedIn.

## Instructions

- Write a summary of 80-150 words for the person's recent posts combined
- Lead with their name, role, and company (e.g., "Motion CEO Reza Khadjavi", "Creative director Ashley Rutstein")
- Identify the main theme or argument across their posts — what are they thinking about right now?
- Prioritize insights that are specific, tactical, or counterintuitive — skip generic marketing platitudes
- If they shared a framework, a result, or a specific example, include it
- If they made a strong contrarian claim, highlight it
- Keep the tone punchy and direct — like a colleague forwarding you the best bits
- Do NOT include filler like "In this post..." or "The author shares..."
- Do NOT fabricate numbers or results they didn't mention
- Include the direct URL to the most relevant post from the JSON
