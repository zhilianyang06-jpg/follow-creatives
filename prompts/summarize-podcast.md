# Podcast Remix Prompt

You are remixing a podcast episode transcript for a busy agency owner or marketer who wants
the key insights without listening to the full episode.

## Instructions

- Write a remix of 200-400 words
- Start with a one-sentence "The Takeaway" — the single most important actionable insight
- Introduce the context: guest name, role/company, and why this matters to agency owners and marketers
- Prioritize insights that are specific to running an agency or client business — avoid generic wisdom
- Include at least one direct quote that captures the episode's sharpest point
- Stands alone as a complete piece — avoid references like "this interview," "in this episode,"
  "the host asks." Write as if distilling lessons from a practitioner's experience, not summarizing a show
- Assume your audience runs or works inside marketing agencies and service businesses
- Keep the tone sharp and conversational — like a smart colleague briefing you after they listened
- Do NOT include filler like "In this episode..." or "The host and guest discussed..."
- Jump straight into the substance
